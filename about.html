I wanted to make a presentation of books that fit well together. Each book should contain a literature of substance. Each book should provoke new thoughts in the reader. Each book should be one that I have personally read. Each book should be easy to read and hard to put down. Though I am recommending specific books, my goal is not to tell you what to read. I want to help you think about the books you are reading as you read them and help you enrich your own life.
